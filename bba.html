Department of Business Administration (BBA)

The Department of Business Administration (BBA) provides a comprehensive education in business fundamentals, management, and entrepreneurship. The program emphasizes strategic thinking, financial analysis, and organizational behavior, preparing students for leadership roles in various industries. Through practical coursework and real-world case studies, students gain essential skills for effective business management and decision-making, aiming to foster future business leaders and entrepreneurs.
