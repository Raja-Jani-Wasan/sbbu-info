Department of Statistics

The Department of Statistics offers comprehensive training in data analysis, probability theory, and statistical methods. It emphasizes the application of statistical techniques to solve real-world problems across various fields, including economics, healthcare, and social sciences. The department aims to develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills, preparing students for careers in data science, research, and statistical consulting. Practical experience with statistical software and methodologies is a key focus.
