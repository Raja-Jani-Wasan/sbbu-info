Department of Genetics

The Department of Genetics focuses on the study of genes, heredity, and genetic variation. It covers topics such as molecular genetics, genetic engineering, and genomics. The department aims to provide students with a deep understanding of genetic principles and their applications in research, medicine, and biotechnology. Practical laboratory experience and advanced research opportunities prepare students for careers in genetic research, clinical genetics, and related fields.
